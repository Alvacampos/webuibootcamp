GAME RULES: The game has 2 player, playing in rounds. In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as he whishes. Each result gets added to his ROUND score. But, if the player rolls a 1, all his ROUND scores get lost. After that, it´s the next player´s turn. The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his ROUND score gets added to his GLOBAL score. After that, it´s the next player´s turn. The first player to reach 100 points on GLOBAL score wins.
